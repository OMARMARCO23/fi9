Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

Commitment to Privacy: SmoothGetup is built with a strict offline-first design philosophy. We do not use user accounts, transmit personal sleep patterns, or utilize external behavioral tracker kits. Your metrics and sleep routines remain entirely yours, stored safely on your device.

1. Application Identification & Governance

This Privacy Policy governs the Android application SmoothGetup (Package Name: com.fi9.smoothwakeupalarm). SmoothGetup is designed to provide optimized wake-up routines, sunrise light transitions, sleep breathing guidelines, and adaptive morning weather alarms.

2. Device Permissions & Core Purpose disclosures

To provide precise and reliable alarm routines without crashing, the application requires several Android system permissions. Below is a detailed disclosure of how these permissions are strictly used:

Exact Alarm Scheduling

Uses the Android Exact Alarm framework to launch your sunrise simulation exactly on schedule. Needed to guarantee alerts trigger when you expect them.

Ringtone & Audio Customization

Accesses standard local device system chimes and ringtones when user requests a specific wake-up song via the Custom Ringtone Selector interface.

Internet & Location Queries

Connects briefly to a public API (api.open-meteo.com) strictly for regional weather-adaptive forecasts. Location values are processed in real-time and never logged.

Wake Lock & Screen Brightness

Allows the glowing display to remain on and scale brightness during sunset winddown or dynamic sunrise simulations so colors stay visible.

3. Zero-Data Collection and Sharing

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, phone numbers, contact books, emails, or unique device MAC coordinates.
  • No Analytics or Ads: There are no third-party telemetry frameworks, performance diagnostics logs, or invasive advertiser SDKs active inside the binary.
  • No Cloud sync database: All settings, designated chimes, and wake schedules are persisted exclusively using the secure Android SQLite/Room database on local user storage.

4. Compliance with Google Play Policies

The app fully complies with the Google Play Developer Distribution Agreement. It incorporates no hidden subscriptions, complies with local laws, protects family privacy thresholds, and represents itself transparently without mock or misleading claims.
